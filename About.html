  2. About Pithoragarh

For its natural beauty and beauty, Uttarakhand has made a different identity in the whole world, a famous district of this state "Pithoragarh" is located in the eastern part of the Himalayas. In which there is supernatural beauty. It is also known as Mini Kashmir. The weather, pure air, natural beauty, food, lifestyle, rivers, peak valleys, waterfalls, glaciers etc. make it even more popular. Pithoragarh district was established on 24 February 1960. Pithoragarh is situated at an altitude of 1645 meters above sea level. Pithoragarh is also known for its dense forests, rich flora and fauna. You will feel like heaven when you reach here. Pithoragarh district shares international border with Tibet in the north and Nepal in the east. Kumaoni, Hindi are the main languages spoken in the region. The city of Pithoragarh/Saur Valley is situated in the middle of four hills Chandak, Dhwaja, Kundar and Thal Kedar. The city is very popular among tourists as old forts, temples, hill stations and as a trekking destination. Grasslands, perennial streams flowing down the mountains, a rich variety of flora and fauna and lush green landscapes invite a visit to the beautiful Pithoragarh. Many deities also reside here. The trek to Kailash Mansarovar also passes through this district, apart from this, the journey of Chupilkedar, which is called the gate of heaven, is also the same, this city has retained its cultural, geographical position. The snow-clad Panchachuli peaks are not only a boon for nature lovers, but also a paradise for adventure sports and tourism, who can indulge in activities like trekking, camping, hiking, overnight stay etc. Pithoragarh is considered a place of natural beauty and peace along with deities.
