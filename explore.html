Immersive Experiences

In the land of opulence, let loose and discover yourself. Perhaps one of the most beautiful and fascinating things about a visit to Pithoragarh in Uttarakhand, the state is full of cultural experiences, people, clothing, dialects, languages, cuisines. And the culture here takes many forms – from the food you eat to the people you spend time with or the beauty of nature hidden in plain sight. Come join us and see Pithoragarh district, which is also known as Chhota Kashmir, situated in the lap of nature. Uttarakhand is a land of misty Himalayas, roaring rivers, serene forests and ancient temples. Without a doubt, it is one of my most favourite states in India. Though the famous Shiva temples (Badrinath & Kedarnath), Nainital, Mussoorie, Haridwar, Rishikesh etc are some of the most common attractions of Uttarakhand; this time I got a chance to explore the hidden gems in Uttarakhand. These offbeat places in Uttarakhand totally left me awestruck. All thanks to Uttarakhand Tourism for this amazing experience.
